Detailed documentation

AppEasy is a camera application engineered that would be able to bring the most popular and frequently used social media applications all into one application. The idea is to be able to authenticate all multiple applications at the same time and to be able to share meida onto the selected app by a single click. Users can upload photos ,videos,Gifs and be able to view comments ,notifications and updates from their favourite social media application all within AppEasy.

Beautiful, modern design

With risp UI and easy navigations.

Easy to set up

A unique single access password to secure all your favourite social networking accounts.

All you want from an app

The Application aims at making it convenient and easy for users to be able to post pictures, videos or GIFSs instantly to a selected amount of social media applications of their choice instantly with just one click .

Capture Video

The app uses the users camera on the device, making use of all camera features.

Cool UI

User interface is simple, and considers people with achromatopsia (color blindness) or Color Vision Deficiency.

Scenarios Mapping

Scenario mapping will help you to think about your users, to think about their tasks and most importantly to think about the sort of user experience you want to provide. It will also help to ensure that your designs are grounded in the real world because scenario mapping forces you to consider the context in which a design is likely to be used.
